Gather: A Collaborative Travel App

During the spring of 2020, with summer quickly approaching, and vacation on my mind I began thinking about how technology has impacted the way we plan travel. What are the major challenges people face while coordinating their next adventures, and could we design a better system to provide them with a more pleasant experience?

The Challenge:

Based on research, I discovered that many travelers want a weekend getaway to recharge, and escape from the grind. They enjoy traveling with companions, and want time to connect with friends, but they’re overwhelmed with all they have to manage on a daily basis, and often don’t have the energy or motivation to enquire about schedules or event planning because it requires so much extra effort and communication.

The Solution:

Travelers need a collaborative network for building itineraries and managing details for current trips and future travel ambitions to help hard working overburdened young professionals and their companions easily coordinate schedules, establish common goals, and manifest their plans.

The Research Phase

The Definition Phase

During the definition phase I analyzed and synthesized the data by assembling all the findings into an affinity diagram. This helped me isolate and highlight valuable key user insights. These insights illuminated the common thread that ran through many of my conversations…traveling with friends is fun, but coordinating schedules leaves a lot to be desired.

User Insight Statement:

A hard working young professional who seeks weekend getaways as a break from the grind needs a convenient way to collect, organize, and coordinate the details of her trip with her travel companions, because the last thing she needs is for planning her escape to feel like a second job.

Problem Statement:

We believe providing hard working young professionals and their companions with a collaborative network for collecting, and managing travel details will help distribute the mental load, cultivate common goals, and set expectations from the outset resulting in a more enjoyable experience for all involved.
